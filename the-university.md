# 🎓 The University – Complete Game Design Document

## (Non-Technical Full Version)

---

## Core Concept

An **interactive psychological drama** set in a university, where you play as a student facing **Julian** – a bully with massive social influence. But Julian isn't "evil" in the traditional sense. He's someone who **needs control**. Anyone new who enters the university finds themselves – unconsciously – under his influence. Everyone joins him because it's the easiest path.

Then you come. You don't resist. You don't join. You're just... not under his influence. And that alone is enough for him to attack you.

**The Essence:** The game isn't about confronting a bully. It's about how you deal with a system that only sees you if you're with it or against it.

---

## The Story

### Part One

You and Maya are a couple in university. Love, study, shared dreams. Then Julian enters the picture. A star athlete, a charming smile hiding a ruthless nature.

**Why does he attack you?** Not because you're weaker. Not because you wronged him. Just because you're **outside his circle**. Anyone new automatically joins him. You didn't. This confuses him. He attacks you to force you to join – or destroy you.

He starts with small insults (cold comments, blocking you in hallways). It escalates to public humiliation (tripping you in the cafeteria, mocking your projects in front of everyone). Maya stands by you at first, but as the humiliation repeats, she begins to waver.

Then Julian turns his attention to Maya. He no longer just wants to hurt you. He wants to take her from you.

---

### Part Two (Potential – depends on Part One's ending)

If you let Maya go with Julian in Part One, she may return in a second part. The relationship between her and Julian is built on convenience, not love. She knows it. Julian knows it. She's trapped, but she's afraid to go back.

**Possible Part Two beginnings:**
- **Revenge Path:** You plan to bring Julian down for good.
- **Restoration Path:** You want to win Maya back, but you don't know if she wants to return.
- **Reconciliation Path:** You decide to confront the past and understand Maya.
- **Escape Path:** You try to convince Maya to escape together, because Julian has become dangerous to her.

## Characters

### Julian – The Bully (Not What He Seems)

- **Role:** Star athlete, social influencer.
- **Personality:** Charismatic, successful, loved. But he needs control.
- **The Reason:** Everyone who enters the university automatically joins him. You didn't. This threatens his world.
- **Weakness:** Afraid that others will discover they don't need him. If his influence is exposed, he crumbles.
- **Behavior:** He attacks you not because he hates you. Because he needs to force you to join – or destroy you.

---

### Maya – Your Girlfriend (Not a Prize)

- **Role:** Your partner at university.
- **Personality:** Independent, but affected by social pressure.
- **Inner Conflict:** Loves you, but begins to waver when she sees how Julian treats you.
- **Two Hidden Metrics:**
  - **Trust in You:** Increases when you defend her intelligently, involve her in decisions, show quiet strength.
  - **Attraction to Julian:** Increases when you appear weak, hesitant, or irrationally jealous.

**Maya is not a prize to be won or lost.** She's an independent character. If you lose her, she chose to leave – not because she loves Julian, but because she got tired of fighting.

---

### The Supporting Characters (6 Characters)

#### Elena – The First Victim
- **Past:** She outperformed Julian on a project. He spread rumors about her cheating.
- **Status:** Isolated, studies alone in the library.
- **How to Attract:** By helping her with a difficult project.

#### Daniel – The Second Victim
- **Past:** Was a close friend of Julian, disagreed with him, and Julian exposed his secrets.
- **Status:** Trusts no one, refuses new friendships.
- **How to Attract:** Through patience, sitting beside him without expectations.

#### Iris – The Third Victim
- **Past:** Joined Julian's circle, tried to warn others, became an outcast.
- **Status:** Hides in the shadows, refuses any social interaction.
- **How to Attract:** By defending her (without embarrassing her) when someone mocks her.

#### Omar – The Neutral
- **Status:** A quiet student, neither with nor against Julian.
- **How to Attract:** By showing you're not an enemy of Julian, but someone worthy of respect.

#### Nadia – The Activist
- **Status:** In the student union, knows that Julian controls some decisions.
- **How to Attract:** By showing you understand the political game at university.

#### Elias – Inside Julian's Circle (An Infiltration Opportunity)
- **Status:** Julian's close friend, feels guilty, wants out but is scared.
- **Weakness:** His younger sister is sick; he needs help.
- **How to Attract:** By helping him with his sister's problem (without mentioning Julian). Or by showing you're not asking him to "betray" Julian – just for a small favor.

## Game Mechanics

### The Four Paths (Determined Through Choices, Not Menus)

| Path | How It Appears | When the Player Chooses It |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Direct Confrontation** | You challenge Julian face to face. | When the player feels angry. |
| **Avoidance** | You avoid places where he is. | When the player feels fear. |
| **Appeasement** | You try to win Julian's favor. | When the player feels powerless. |
| **Indirect Confrontation** (Smart) | You build alliances, gather information, expose him in a way that can't be traced back. | When the player is strategic. |

**The player doesn't choose a "path" from a menu. The path is determined through their small decisions.**

---

### Small Tactics (Indirect Confrontation)

| Tactic | How the Player Executes It | Effect on Julian |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Smirk** | After excelling in an exam, you glance at Julian with a slight smile. | He sees the challenge. He can't accuse you of anything. |
| **Correcting Julian Gently** | Say: "I think the answer is different... shall we check the source together?" | He's embarrassed. He can't respond (because the tone is polite). |
| **Deflecting His Sarcasm** | Say: "Well, that's one perspective." (Without emotion). | He feels his weapon isn't working. |
| **The Question That Confuses Him** | In a group discussion: "Julian, how can your idea be applied in a different context?" | Exposes his ignorance if he answers poorly. |
| **Casual Greeting** | Walk past him, wave, say: "Julian." Then walk on. | He feels like he's been "walked past." |

---

### The "Impact" System (Hidden)

A hidden metric from 0 to 100 (not visible to the player), changing based on their tactics and choices.

| Threshold | Change in the Game |
| :--- | :--- |
| 10 | A stranger asks for your help studying. |
| 20 | A professor praises your participation. Julian looks at you impatiently. |
| 35 | One of Julian's friends asks to speak with you secretly. |
| 50 | A group of students invites you to a study session (without Julian). |
| 65 | Julian tries to get close to you (temporarily). |
| 80 | Maya says: "I noticed you've changed. You seem more confident." |
| 100 | Julian breaks down (publicly or privately). |

---

### The Trait System (7 Traits Formed by Choices)

| Trait | Side A | Side B |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Confrontation** | Direct | Indirect |
| **Self-Defense** | Steadfast | Reactive |
| **Handling Pressure** | Calm | Anxious |
| **Relationship with Maya (Behavior)** | Protective | Partner |
| **Social Reputation** | Outgoing | Reserved |
| **Sense of Humor** | Sarcastic | Warm |
| **Empathy** | Sensitive | Balanced |

**Traits are not permanent.** They can be changed slowly through different choices. Never shown to the player.

---

### Maya's Relationship – Two Hidden Metrics

| Metric | Increases When... | Decreases When... |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Trust in You** | You listen to her, share your feelings, defend her intelligently | You ignore her feelings, act angrily, get jealous without reason |
| **Attraction to Julian** | You appear weak, hesitant, lacking self-confidence | You act with quiet confidence, build your reputation |

**Maya's Main Scenes (6 Scenes):**

1. After an insult from Julian: Do you share your feelings with her or ignore them?
2. Maya talks about Julian's invitation: Do you forbid her, allow her, or ask "Do you want to go?"
3. After you outperform Julian: Do you brag, stay humble, or share the success with her?
4. Maya doubts your relationship: Do you reassure her, avoid her, or confront her?
5. Maya tells you a rumor about you: Do you deflect, ask "Do you believe me?", or reveal the truth?
6. Maya tells you about Julian's words (through Nadia): Do you deflect, ask "Do you believe him?", or be honest with her?

## Endings (7 Endings)

| Ending | Condition | The Bitter Taste |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Victory** (Julian breaks down) | Impact ≥ 80, strong network, Maya with you | You felt emptiness, not happiness |
| **Escape** (You leave the university) | Impact ≥ 40, weak network | You left her behind |
| **Loss** (You lose Maya) | Maya: Trust ≤ 30, Attraction to Julian ≥ 70 | You didn't fight enough |
| **Integration** (You join Julian) | Appeasement path, Impact ≤ 20 | You know you betrayed yourself |
| **Breakdown** (You psychologically collapse) | Impact ≤ 20, constant avoidance | You left before you were thrown out |
| **Successful Exposure** (Indirect confrontation) | Impact ≥ 90, full network | Some may never forgive you |
| **Draw** (No one wins) | Medium Impact (40-60) | You didn't settle anything |

### Maya's Reactions to Bad Endings

**In the Loss Ending:**
> **Maya:** "I loved you. Really. But I can't stay in a battle I don't understand."

**In the Integration Ending:**
> **Maya:** "Are you really happy?"

**In the Breakdown Ending:**
> **Maya (message):** "I'm sorry. But I can't save you from yourself."

## Conclusion

**The University** is not a game about bullying.  
It's not a game about success or failure.  
It's not a game about "defeating the villain."

**It's a game about influence.** About how social systems work. About how one person can be "outside the circle" – and that alone is enough to be attacked.

Every choice has a price. Every path has an ending. Every ending has a taste of loss.

**You don't defeat Julian with your strength. You defeat him by showing that his influence isn't the only one.**
